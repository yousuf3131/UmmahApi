Endpoints

Qibla

Find prayer direction to Makkah from any coordinates.

GET /api/qibla?lat={lat}&lng={lng} Try (Chicago)

Prayer Times

Accurate Fajr–Isha times with multiple calculation methods.

GET /api/prayer-times?lat={lat}&lng={lng}&date=YYYY-MM-DD&method=MuslimWorldLeague Try (NYC)

Hijri Date

Gregorian → Hijri conversion and Islamic events.

GET /api/hijri-date?date=YYYY-MM-DD Try

Asma ul Husna

99 Names of Allah with meanings.

GET /api/asma-ul-husna Try

Quick Examples

curl "https://ummahapi.com/api/qibla?lat=41.8781&lng=-87.6298"

curl "https://ummahapi.com/api/prayer-times?lat=24.8607&lng=67.0011&date=2025-09-19&method=Karachi"

Limits & Fair Use

  • Calculations: 30 calculations / minute
  • Protection: 1000 requests / hour
